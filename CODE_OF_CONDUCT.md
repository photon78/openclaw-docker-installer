# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Show empathy

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behaviour

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
